Call scripts

Opener
"Hey, is this the owner?" (If NO → Script D)

"Good morning/afternoon — how is your day?"

"Listen — I'll be honest, this is a sales call. Do you want to hang up on me, or do you want to give me 30 seconds?"
Pitch
"So I actually went ahead and took a risk — I already built you a free website. It's free to look at. And if you like it, I'm only asking for $500. If you don't like it, you never have to hear from me ever again. How does that sound?"
If hesitant
"Out of curiosity — how are people finding your business right now?"

"That's exactly the gap a website fills — people searching online at midnight who'd never find you otherwise."
If NO / hesitate
"Totally fair — most professional web agencies charge $3,000 to $10,000+ upfront, and you're still waiting weeks to see anything. I already built your demo for free — you only pay if you love it. If you want to look — would you rather a direct link or a quick call?"
Opener
"Hello — is this the owner?" (If NO → Script D)

"Hi — this might be your least favorite call of the day. It's a cold call — I'll keep it to 20 seconds, or you could hang up right now." (wait for yes)
Hook
"Here's why I called — I noticed your business doesn't have a website yet, and I actually built you a free demo so you can see what you're missing before you spend anything."
Offer
"So I already built you a free website. Free to look at. If you like it, I'm only asking for $500. If you don't like it, you never have to hear from me again. How does that sound?"
If NO / hesitate
"Totally fair — professional agencies usually charge $3,000 to $10,000+ and you wait forever. Yours is already built — you only pay if you love it. Direct link or a quick meeting to walk through it?"
Opener
"Hey, is this the business owner?" (If NO → Script D)

"Hey, my name is [Your Name] — I'm a student locally and I run a small website design business on the side. I built a free personalized landing page for your business."
Qualify + offer
"Do you have anything online right now, or mostly word-of-mouth?" … "That's why I made this — so people can find you." … Same $500 free-demo offer as other scripts.
Get transferred
"Hi — may I speak to the owner?" … "Hi, my name is [Your Name] — student locally studying web design. I noticed your business doesn't have a website. Is there a reason for that?"
Offer
Same free website / $500 pitch. If interested → three questions (link vs meeting, phone OK, name).
Opener
"Hey, quick question — do you want the good news or the bad news first?" … "Good news — I built a free custom landing page for your business. Bad news — you're probably missing sales because people can't find you online."
Offer
Qualify how customers find them → same free demo / $500 offer.

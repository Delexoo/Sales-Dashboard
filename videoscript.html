The Business

Who are we

Who are we?

We are a Website Agency that builds and sells websites to local businesses.

Even in 2026, many plumbers, salons, restaurants, house cleaners, chiropractors, contractors, and other local businesses still don't have a website. Because of that, they're losing potential customers every day.

Without a website, customers can't easily find them online, learn about their services, book appointments, or even contact them.

A website is often a customer's first impression of a business. That's the problem we solve.

We focus on local businesses that either don't have a website or have an outdated one, and we help them build a stronger online presence.

Your role

What’s your role?

You are a cold caller, you are the first point of contact.

Your job is to call local businesses, get them interested in the free demo, and pass qualified leads to us.

You are not a web designer, developer, or account manager. You focus on the conversation which is pretty simple if you ask me.

We handle the build, bookings, client texts, payment, delivery, and everything else on the backend.

What do we sell

What do we sell?

We first build a free demo so business owners can see the value themselves and see what they are missing. If they want to go live, that’s when they pay the price.

Most professional web agencies charge from $3,000 - $20,000. Our rates are significantly lower, we only charge from $500-$1,500.

How this business works

I’m not here to hide anything, so let me show you something pretty interesting.

There are three major parts that keep this business running: You, Me, and the Bot.

Your role is simple: you reach out to local businesses and see if they're interested in a website. You forward their information to the bot.

The bot then validates the lead to make sure the information is accurate and legitimate. Once verified, it will generate a customized website using my own system and sends the website preview directly to the business owner.

From there, I take over. I communicate with the business owner, answer any questions, schedule a 1-on-1 meeting if needed, handle payments, and make sure everything runs smoothly from start to finish.

What's interesting is that because the bot isn't a person, there isn't a third team member taking a share of the revenue. That's why we're able to split the earnings nearly 50/50 between you and me, rather than dividing it among three people at roughly 30% each.

How you get paid

How you get paid,

You call a business → submit their information to the bot → I close the deal.

Once the transaction is completed and payment is received, your dashboard will be updated automatically and I forward your commission through your preferred payment method. You earn the full 40% of the sale.

Everyday Tasks (In action)

This is your real workday loop · six steps, same order every time. I’ll walk through each one on screen so you can follow along while you work.

1 · Open Lead Finder

Start in Lead Finder. This is our list of local businesses · most of them don’t have a website yet, which is who we want.

If you want the best fits, use the No website filter so you’re only looking at businesses that need us the most.

Once you’re in, pick one business to work · that’s step two.

2 · Pick a business

Scroll the list and choose one business to call today. Don’t try to work ten at once · one lead, one conversation, one outcome.

On that card, click Build Lead. That pins the business for you and sends a lot of the details into Lead Builder so you’re not typing everything from scratch later.

Now you’re ready to call them · step three.

3 · Call & pitch the website

Dial from the lead card, or use the phone number shown. Open Call scripts if you want a script on screen while you talk.

Your pitch is simple: we build a free demo website so they can see what they’re missing. You’re not selling a $5,000 agency package · you’re offering a no-risk preview.

If they’re not interested, thank them, hang up, and go back to step two with a different business. Do not post that lead.

If they are interested, keep going · step four.

4 · If interested

Fill out the Lead Builder · you should already have fields filled from Build Lead. Double-check everything matches what you said on the call.

The price has to match what you quoted. The Google Maps link should be there. Add phone, owner name, and whether they want a direct link or booking setup.

When it’s accurate, click Send lead · step five.

5 · Send lead in Lead Builder

In Lead Builder, click Send lead when every field is filled. Your submission is saved on the website right away · while the call is still fresh.

After you send, come back to Lead Finder for step six.

6 · Mark the business as complete

Back in Lead Finder, tag that business Complete so the team knows you finished this one. Everyone can see Complete · it’s a team mark.

If you still need to call them back later, use Pending instead. Pending is yours until you finish the follow-up.

Then start again at step two: pick the next business, call, post if they’re interested, mark Complete. That’s the loop · repeat it every workday.

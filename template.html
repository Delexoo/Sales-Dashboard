How to use Lead Builder

Work top to bottom. Each row in the form matches a step below.

  1. Business name Build Lead from Lead Finder fills this in for you. Otherwise, enter the business name from your call.
    Business Name Bobby's Burgers
  2. Price You choose the price · use what you quoted on the call. Build Lead may suggest a tier from review count; change it anytime.
    Price $500 $700 $1,000 $1,500
  3. Google Maps Build Lead from Lead Finder fills this in for you. Otherwise, paste the business's Google Maps link.
    Google Maps maps.google.com/…
  4. Phone Build Lead auto-fills this from the lead card. Otherwise, enter the owner's phone number. Tap Call to dial again if needed.
    Phone 4013000957 Call
  5. Preference Ask on the call. Direct Link = they get a text with the demo link · Booking = they schedule a meeting to learn more. You don't host · I do.
    Preference Direct Link Booking
  6. Owner name Ask the owner for their name when you call. First name is fine · last name only if they give one.
    Owner Name Mike
  7. Send When you're done, click Send lead. Your submission is saved · no copy-paste needed. We handle the rest — we create the website, send it, collect payment, and host the meeting with the business owner. You just sit back and relax · we'll notify you when your commission is ready!
